Frequently Asked Question

Do you offer both local and international tours?
We offer a wide range of local tours within Nigeria and exciting international travel experiences. Whether you’re looking for a cultural adventure, a beach escape, or a luxury getaway, there’s something for everyone. Simply explore our available trips and we’ll take it from there!
How do I book a tour or travel package?
Once you find a package you love, just click the WhatsApp button on the tour page. One of our travel consultants will assist you with availability, pricing, and next steps. We’ll handle everything from there ,giving you a stress-free booking experience directly on WhatsApp.
What is included in the tour package price?
Our tour packages typically include: Accommodation, Breakfast, Tour guides & planned activities, Return tickets. Some packages may include flights or travel insurance, while others may not. A full breakdown of what's included will be shared once you connect with us on WhatsApp , so there are no hidden surprises!
Is there a deposi to secure my booking?
Yes, a 30% deposit i to secure your booking. This ensures your spot is reserved and the necessary arrangements are made in advance. The remaining balance can be paid at a later date, as advised by our team. Once you're ready, click the WhatsApp button on the package page and we’ll guide you through the payment process smoothly.
Do you help with visa applications?
Yes, we do! If your chosen destination requires a visa, our team can assist you with the entire visa application process. We’ll provide a checklist o documents, help you prepare, and offer guidance to increase your chances of approval. Please note that visa decisions are made by the embassies, but we’re here to support you every step of the way.
